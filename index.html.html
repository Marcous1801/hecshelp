How it works

Three steps, zero effort

01

Link your bank

Connect your everyday spending account. We use read-only access — we can see your transactions but never touch your money without permission.

02

Spend normally

Every purchase gets rounded up to the nearest dollar. We track the difference silently in the background — no action needed from you.

03

Watch debt shrink

Once your round-ups hit $5, we send a BPAY payment directly to your HECS account. You get a notification. That's it.
